FAQ

How do I start playing?

Simply download the game and tap on 'Play' to begin.

Are there any in-game purchases?

Yes, there are optional purchases for special power-ups.

Can I play without an internet connection?

Yes, you can play offline without any issues.

How do I get past challenging levels?

Try using power-ups and thinking ahead for better combos.

Is there a way to compete with friends?

Yes, you can connect to social media to see friends' scores.

What are the rewards for playing?

Earn gems, power-ups, and other special items as you progress.

Is the game suitable for children?

Absolutely, it's designed to be family-friendly.
